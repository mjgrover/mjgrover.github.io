IBM Data and AI Learning Product Skills Validation Digital Badge Program

Knowledge badge - candidate to pass one test.

Proficiency badge - candidate to earn the necessary (two or more) Knowledge badges.

Skill badge - candidate to earn the necessary (two or more) Knowledge badges. These badges do not qualify business partners.

Note: badges are issued automatically within 24 hours.
